Crime Heatmap

Using the crime heatmap is is easy to visualize the levels of crime during specific times of the days in the week. Using this map as a visualization, it is clear to see that during certain times of the week such as Friday night a large amount of crime takes place. It can also be seen that Thursday morning has very little crime.

Train Schedule

Using this visualization it is easy to see the time that a train takes to move from station to station. Hovering over a given train line offers more information about that given line. When hovering, the train highlights its path, marks the times of station stops via vertical lines and a tooltip pops up which outlines the entire train path by showing the stations and the times that they are stopped at.
